My Musical Journey

My Experience with Piano

I began playing piano at the age of 8 and have continued to develop my skills throughout my academic career.

Classical Training

My foundation in piano is rooted in classical training. I've performed pieces by Bach, Beethoven, Chopin, and Debussy. The technical precision required for classical piano has been beneficial for my analytical thinking in my engineering studies.

Jazz Exploration

In recent years, I've developed an interest in jazz piano. The improvisational nature of jazz has helped me approach problem-solving in my research with more creativity and flexibility. I particularly enjoy exploring jazz harmonies and modal improvisation.

Favorite Composers & Pieces

  • Bach: Well-Tempered Clavier
  • Chopin: Nocturnes
  • Debussy: Clair de Lune
  • Bill Evans: Jazz improvisations
